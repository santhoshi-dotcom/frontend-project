About RePlate

RePlate is a Zero Waste Food Donation System designed to reduce food waste and fight hunger. Every day, tons of food are wasted while many people struggle without proper meals.

Our platform connects food donors such as restaurants, hotels, and individuals with NGOs and people in need. By donating surplus food, we ensure that good food reaches the right hands instead of ending up in landfills.

Our mission is simple: " Don't waste food — donate it to someone in need ❤️."
